# This guide is intended to walk an average user through the process of manually setting up three Azure Free-tier Linux virtual machines. These systems will serve as blank servers/workstations in other IT related projects. 
# Modern IT infrastructure has a significant impact on the way businesses operate. It is crucial for IT professionals and organizations as it provides the foundation for building scalable, cost-effective, and resilient IT infrastructures that can adapt to the dynamic needs of the business environment.

# Step 1
# Navigate to https://azure.microsoft.com/en-us/free#all-free-services and click on the "Start Free" green botton. 

# Step 2 
# Assuming you don't already have a Microsoft account, click on the "Create one!" botton. 

# Step 3
# Sign up with your desired email and new password. Follow the email verification and CAPTCHA puzzle.
